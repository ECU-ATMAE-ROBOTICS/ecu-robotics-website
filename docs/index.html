About ECU Robotics

The ECU ATMAE Robotics Team is a student-led organization dedicated to advancing robotics technology through hands-on projects, competitions, and community outreach.

We bring together students from various disciplines including engineering, computer science, and technology to collaborate on innovative robotics solutions. Our team participates in regional competitions while also working on projects that are used for college recruitment events.
